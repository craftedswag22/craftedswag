Frequently Asked Questions

Hopefully here you can find answers to your potential questions. If you do not, please feel free to contact me.

Question Answer
How long does it take to create my piece? The length of time depends on the order size. If you are ordering one piece, it can take 2-3 days. If you order more pieces, it could take a week or longer.
How is the price determined? The price depends on the material and the product cost as well as the time it takes to complete. Contact me for a quote.
Can I get my order shipped? For now, I am a local business only in the Phoenix Valley area with options of pick up or drop off.
